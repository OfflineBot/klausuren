Pareto Front Explorer

Two decision-space plots show the objective landscapes over the same decision variables. Samples are linked across all views, and Pareto-optimal samples are highlighted.

What you are seeing

Both objectives are minimized. A sample is Pareto-optimal if no other sampled point is at least as good in both objectives and strictly better in one. The lower curve in objective space approximates the sampled Pareto front.
